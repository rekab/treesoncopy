JACOB DIDAS

Ensemble

Jacob Didas is an artist and producer from Buffalo, New York. He left a career as a psychiatric nurse in the Army to pursue his passion in music. His style frequently shifts between genres, the foremost being hip hop. He produces and records independently, creating a sound that's psychedelic and ever-evolving. Jacob's songs seek to spread a message of interconnection and provide a means of healing and escape. Visit www.jacobdidasmusic.com to listen.
