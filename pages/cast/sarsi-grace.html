SARSI GRACE

Wind

Originally from Perth, Australia, Sarsi studied at the Western Australian Academy of Performing Arts before moving to the UK to study Musical Theatre at RTA. She performed as Woman 2 in Side by Side by Sondheim, Shell Dockley in Bad Girls and most recently, Zoran in an off West End performance of Legends of Arahma. In-training credits include Ulla in The Producers and Miss Fleming in Heathers.
