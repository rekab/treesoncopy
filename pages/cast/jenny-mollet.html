JENNY MOLLET

Activist (Rowan)

Jenny Mollet is an actor, singer, and dancer originally from Switzerland but is based in New York City. Jenny has appeared in the original Broadway production of Oprah Winfrey's, "The Color Purple" as Young Celie/Young Olivia/Henrietta and is currently in the 50th Anniversary Broadway National Tour of "Jesus Christ Superstar," as Ensemble/Soul Singer/ u/s Mary Magdalene. She has performed all over the country in regional theater, readings, workshops, and on television as a dancer. She was also the Associate Choreographer and a dancer in Tituss Burgess' music video for his hit single "45. Jenny is a recent graduate of the BFA Musical Theater program at University of Cincinnati College Conservatory of Music (CCM). For more information: @jenny.mollet or jennymollet.com
