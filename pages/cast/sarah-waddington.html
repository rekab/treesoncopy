SARAH WADDINGTON

Holly

Originally from Sydney, Sarah is based in Perth, Australia. In 2010, at the age of 16, Sarah attained her AMEB Grade 6 Certificate in Classical Singing at the Sydney Conservatorium of Music. Now, on the verge of becoming a qualified Australian Lawyer, the COVID-19 global pandemic has inspired Sarah to finally pursue her passion for singing, performance and musical theatre. Sarah is also married and has two young children. In her spare time, you will find Sarah posting covers to YouTube, volunteering in the community, cooking and entertaining family and friends. Please visit www.sarahwaddingtonmusic.com.
