STEPHEN INGRAM

Activist (Wren)

Stephen is a Canadian actor, singer, musician, composer, and music director from Hamilton, ON. He graduated from Western University's Don Wright Faculty of Music and has performed professionally in theatres and schools across Ontario. Recent credits include: Juno's Reward (Grand Theatre), Summer of '69 (Victoria Playhouse Petrolia), The Haunting of Hungerford House, Aleck Bell (Tweed and Company Theatre), Glory Days (Upper Canada Playhouse) and Plaid Tidings (Sudbury Theatre Centre).
