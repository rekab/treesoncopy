ALY CARRIGAN

Ensemble

After conquering her studies in New York (both opera and musical theatre), Aly is excited to continue her journey into the theatre world. Performing in shows such as Legally Blonde (New York Film Academy) Young Frankenstein (Pine Tree Players) and Trouble in Tahiti (University of Alberta), she is ready to continue to learn and grow and find the next project! Check out her site for more info: www.alycarrigan.com.
