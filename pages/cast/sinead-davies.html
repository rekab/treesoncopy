SINEAD DAVIES

Fire

Hailing from Galway in the West of Ireland, Sinead has been living and working in London for the last seven years. Here, Sinead played characters ranging from Imogen in Cymbeline to Mrs. Medlock in The Secret Garden. She is passionate about new musical theatre writing and has been involved in the development of Dominic Powell and Lakeisha Lynch-Stevens' Legend and more recently with Ashley M A Walsh and Estee Stimler's The Attic. Always wanting to better her craft, she has now started a new adventure in Cardiff, training for her Masters of Musical Theatre at the prestigious Royal Welsh College of Music and Drama. She is excited to see where this thrilling venture shall take her next.
