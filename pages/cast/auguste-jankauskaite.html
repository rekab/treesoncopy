AUGUSTE JANKAUSKAITE

Water / Activist

Auguste has been in love with music her whole life. She started playing the violin at the age of seven, followed by piano and guitar. She discovered her passion for theatre in 2014 when she was cast as Sheila in the musical Hair. In 2017, she created her own musical theatre ensemble and did numerous small productions. Today, Auguste attends the SAMTS in Zurich in order to receive professional training in musical theatre. In addition to that, she is part of the Zurich Gospel Choir and was recently cast in the Swiss revival of the Broadway musical On Your Feet.
