DANIELE ALAN-CARTER

Ash

Originally from Italy, Daniele has worked internationally in theater, musical theatre and film. He debuted at age 14 as Romeo in the Pop Opera Romeo and Juliet. He trained at the National Academy of Dramatic Arts "Silvio D'Amico" in Rome. Recently he performed as Juan in Altar Boyz and in The 12 Tenors. Other roles include John Darling in Peter Pan - the Musical Alfred in Le Bal Des Vampires directed by Roman Polanski, and the Waiter in For Reasons that Remain Unclear.
