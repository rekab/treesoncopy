DIANA CHU

Activist (Aspen)

Diana Chu is a singer and pianist, teaching a private studio of over 30 piano and voice students in her home in Mission Viejo, California, as well as performing weekly at Holy Trinity Catholic Church in Ladera Ranch. She also performs at several weddings and events every year. She resides with her husband Victor and her children, Violet and Davin. Please visit www.dianalimusic.com. Thanks to Vicki for this fun opportunity to sing in this unique musical!
