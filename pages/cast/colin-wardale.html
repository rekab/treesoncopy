COLIN WARDALE

Don

Colin has sung all his life, although never professionally. As a child he sang with the Liverpool Cathedral Choir then graduated to play lead tenor roles on the amateur operatic stage. He has busked, performed at the legendary Cavern Club and was a member of the Royal Liverpool Philharmonic Choir, including supporting artists such as Bryn Terfel in Puccini's Tosca. Recently he has been recording and live-streaming solo concerts featuring music across a wide range of genres on social media platforms. Check out his work at his YouTube Channel: www.youtube.com/user/colinwardale
